Your Health
Questions Answered

How do I schedule an appointment with a specialist?

You can request an appointment online through our patient portal, call our concierge line at +1 (800) 567-8900, or visit our appointment center on the first floor. Most specialists are available within 48 hours.

Do you accept international insurance plans?

Yes, we accept most major international insurance plans and offer concierge support for medical travelers. Our international patient services team assists with insurance verification, travel arrangements, and accommodation.

What is your visitation policy?

We offer flexible visitation hours from 10 AM to 8 PM daily. Up to two visitors per patient are welcome. Special accommodations available for ICU and pediatric units.

Do you offer second opinion services?

Our second opinion program connects you with board-certified specialists who review your diagnosis and treatment plan. Both in-person and virtual consultations available.
