QUESTIONS?

Frequently asked questions

Immediate answers to our most common inquiries

How do I schedule an appointment?

Call our 24/7 helpline at +1 (800) 567-8900, use the patient portal, or complete the contact form above. Most specialties offer appointments within 48 hours.

Do you accept my insurance?

We are in-network with 50+ national and international insurers. Our financial navigators can verify your coverage within 1 hour.

Is interpretation available?

Yes — we offer on-site and remote interpreters in over 80 languages, including ASL. Please indicate your preference when booking.

What is your emergency wait time?

As a Level 1 Trauma Center, our median door‑to‑provider time is under 15 minutes. Critical cases are seen immediately.

I'm traveling from abroad — what support do you offer?

Our International Concierge team coordinates visas, airport transfers, lodging, and interpretation. Email concierge@stjohns.health.

How do I request my medical records?

Complete the HIPAA release form in the Patient Portal. Digital records are delivered within 3 business days.
